Vocational Excellence

More Than Just a Degree
Real World Skills

At St. Michael's VHSE, we bridge the gap between education and employment. Unlike traditional schools, our curriculum integrates academic learning with professional skill training aligned with the NSQF (National Skills Qualifications Framework).

We specialize in two core industry sectors: Medical Laboratory Technology and Information Technology. Our students graduate not just with a Plus-Two certificate, but with industry-recognized credentials and mandatory On-the-Job Training (OJT).

  • Mandatory On-Job Training (OJT)
  • NSQF Level 4 Certification
  • Direct Link to Professional Colleges
